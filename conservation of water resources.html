Methods Of Water Conservation



Every one is wasting a big resource of water can you find what it is ....Yes RAIN .We could collect rainwater so we could use it for gardening or washing purposes.



Rainwater harvesting is one of the best method used for conserving water. There are different methods used to preserve rainwater instead of getting it wasted.



In dry regions with high rates of evoparation, use drip irrigation or trickle irrigation it is very useful.



The canals used for irrigation field should be properly lined to minimise losses through seepage .



Sprinklers effectively irrigate the area by checking water losses through seepage and evoparation.
