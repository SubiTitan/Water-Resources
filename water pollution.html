Factors of water pollution

  • Human dumping. Due to human dumping of waste and other harmful substances into river water directly.

  • Industrial waste release. Most industries release their processing waste into the rivers.

  • Land Contamination. Contamination during rains due to the flow of rainwater from contaminated land into rivers.

  • Due to air pollution. Air pollution is formed by the disposal of harmful gases into the air.

  • Water pollution damages human health, makes our living conditions precarious and threatens life on the planet.

  • This pollution makes the water unfit to drink and also harmful for uses in other daily needs.
